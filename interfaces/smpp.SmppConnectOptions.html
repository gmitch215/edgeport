    Optionaltls

    tls?: "off" | "implicit"

    Transport security:

    • 'off' (default): plaintext TCP.
    • 'implicit': TLS from the first byte (typically port 3550 / a carrier-specified port).

    SMPP has no in-band STARTTLS, so only plaintext and implicit TLS are offered.
